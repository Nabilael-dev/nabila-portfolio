About Me

I am an educator and aspiring instructional designer passionate about creating learning experiences that are clear, engaging, and accessible. My focus is on using technology, linguistics, and creativity to make education more effective for children and young professionals in Morocco.

Building on my background in English Studies and additional training in Data Analytics and TEFL, I design projects that address real educational needs. Current initiatives include:

  • Developing a blended English learning program for kids, supported by a mobile practice app
  • Writing a bilingual communication skills e-book for young professionals in Morocco
  • Designing digital tools that make learning interactive and easy to access

I enjoy transforming complex ideas into practical solutions through curriculum, digital learning tools, and applied research. My work combines education, technology, and empathy to ensure that learning is both effective and inclusive.
